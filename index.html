SEVENTEEN

SEVENTEEN's HISTORY

Seventeen (Hangul: 세븐틴; write as SEVENTEEN or SVT) is a group idol from South Korean under Pledis Entertainment in 2015. The group consists of thirteen mambers divided into three sub-units, each with a different skill:a 'Hip=Hop Unit', 'Vocal Unit', and 'Perfomance Unit'. Seventeen has earned a reputation as a "self-producing" idol group, with the members being actively involved in the songwriting and ohter aspects of the group.
Seventeen's Unit

Hip-Hop Unit

Hip-hop unit is a four member sub-unit of Seventeen under Pledis Entertaiment. The group consist of SCoups, Wonwoo, Mingyu, and Vernon. They had position as rapper.
Music

You Made My Dawn

You Made My Dawn is the sixth extended play by South Korean boy band Seventeen. It was released on January 21, 2019, through Pledis Entertainment. It debuted atop the Gaon Album Chart, Oricon Albums Chart and at number four on the US Billboard World Albums chart.

CARAT

Seventeen uses the symbol of a diamond a lot, and a carat is a measurement of certain stones and pearls, like diamonds. You may have heard the phrase “14 carat gold” before, well, the exact same measurement applies to diamonds in jewelry. Since Seventeen are the diamond, the fans (carats) are the weight of the diamond, the thing that measures how brilliant the diamond is. Just a thought! It can also be for “17 carat diamond,” which probably isn’t an actual measurement they use, but is convenient because of the group’s name. Below is their logo, which is representing a diamond.
